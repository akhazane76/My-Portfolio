Sharrvesh Raguraman

Creative technologist, multimedia designer, and emerging filmmaker based in Brisbane. I blend hardware, storytelling, and Southeast Asian cultural textures to craft immersive, emotionally resonant experiences. Whether prototyping sensor-driven wearables or shaping visual narratives, I design with intention and atmosphere.

Creative Philosophy

I thrive in collaborative environments where adaptability is key. From soldering under pressure to reworking shotlists mid-shoot, I treat every challenge as a creative constraint. My approach is iterative, resilient, and deeply invested in authenticity—both technical and emotional.

My work is grounded in Southeast Asian symbolism, ambient sound design, and close-up cinematography. I value emotional intimacy, cultural nuance, and strategic co-creation. This portfolio is a reflection of that process: layered, experimental, and always evolving.

Collaborating with peers, mentors, and makerspaces—this is where resilience and creativity meet.
